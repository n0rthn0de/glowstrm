Thought Doula

You have something to say. It just isn't on the page yet.

A listening-first consultation for the idea that hasn't found its form.

Get in touch

Some things don't need editing. They need space.

A Thought Doula session is for the moment before the writing — when you know what you're trying to do but can't find the thread. When the idea is real but the page is blank. When you've been circling the same paragraph for a week and something underneath isn't resolved yet.

You bring what you have. A half-formed idea. A stuck proposal. A project that keeps resisting articulation. A feeling that the work you're doing is more than you know how to say.

Sharéa brings a listening ear, the right questions, and the ability to hear what's underneath what you're saying — and reflect it back in a form you can work with.

What she does in the session: Listens. Asks. Takes notes. Finds the shape that was already there. Hands it back to you.

What she doesn't do: Write it for you. Edit your draft. Replace the work. The Thought Doula session creates the conditions for you to do the work. That's the point.

What you leave with

Not a deliverable in the traditional sense. What you leave with is:

  • Clarity about what the thing actually is
  • A named direction for the next step
  • Notes from the session — in your words, organized
  • The specific sentence or frame that unlocks what comes next (when it exists — and it usually does)

Who this is for

This session is for someone who:

  • Has something real to say and can't get it out of their head and onto the page
  • Is preparing something specific — a pitch, a proposal, a relaunch, a hard conversation — and needs a thinking partner before they start writing
  • Knows their work matters but keeps losing the thread when they try to explain it
  • Has been stuck on the same piece of communication for longer than makes sense

Available by referral or recommendation.

The Thought Doula isn't open enrollment. It's offered to people who come by way of relationship — through someone who knows the work, through a previous engagement, or through a direct recommendation from Sharéa herself.

If you think it might be the right fit, reach out directly. Tell her what you're working on and where you're stuck. She'll let you know whether it makes sense.

Price: $150–$200 depending on session scope. Confirmed at booking.
