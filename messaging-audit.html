What you receive

What this review is — and isn't

This is not a grammar check. Not a proofread. Not a line edit. GlowStrm is not reading your piece for sentence structure — GlowStrm is reading it for what it's actually doing, and whether that matches what you need it to do.

The review is global. Big picture. Message level. The question isn't whether the writing is clean — it's whether the message is landing. If there's a gap between what you mean and what your audience is hearing, that's what you'll walk away knowing. And that's what the recommendations are built to close.

  • What the piece is doing right now What message it's actually communicating — which may or may not be what you intended. Named plainly.
  • Where the gap is The distance between what you meant and what lands — and specifically what's creating that distance.
  • The underlying issue Most message problems have a structural cause: an unclear purpose, an audience mismatch, a thread that fractures under pressure. GlowStrm names the root — not just the symptom.
  • Specific recommendations Not abstract direction — concrete language and approach suggestions that move the piece toward what you need it to do.
  • A clear next step Whether that's a targeted revision, a deeper engagement, or a completely different approach — you leave with a direction, not just feedback.
