What you receive

What this review is — and isn't

This is not a grammar check. Not a proofread. Not a line edit. GlowStrm is not reading your piece for sentence structure — GlowStrm is reading it for what it's actually doing, and whether that matches what you need it to do.

The question isn't whether the writing is clean — it's whether the message is landing.

This review applies GlowStrm's Message Architecture™ methodology to a single piece — the same framework that underlies the full sprint, focused on what you have in front of you right now.

You'll receive:

  • What the piece is doing right now What message it's actually communicating, which may or may not be what you intended. Named plainly. Message-level, not sentence-level.
  • Where the gap is The likely gap between what you mean and what your audience is actually hearing, and specifically what's creating that distance.
  • The underlying issue Most message problems have a structural cause: an unclear purpose, an audience mismatch, a thread that fractures under pressure. GlowStrm names the root — not just the symptom.
  • Specific recommendations Concrete language and approach suggestions that move the piece toward what you need it to do. Not abstract direction.
  • A clear next step Whether that's a targeted revision, a deeper engagement, or a completely different approach. You leave with a direction, not just feedback.

If there's a gap between what you mean and what your audience is hearing, that's what you'll walk away knowing. That's what the recommendations are built to close.
